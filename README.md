# Arduino Bluetooth controlled Car - 2WD



## Components
- Arduino Uno (or similar)
- 2 DC motors 3-6v
- 2 wheels (usually sold together with the motors)
- 1 small protoboard
- 1 2WD chassis
- 1 case for 6 batteries (1.5v each)
- 1 HC-05 (ZS-040) Bluetooth module
- Lots of jumpers
